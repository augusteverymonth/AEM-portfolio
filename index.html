Who I am

I thrive on ambition, good design, art and curiosity, leading an adventurous life full of travel and creativity with my work being reflective of these experiences.

I am currently living in Charlotte,NC, but can often be found in other places all around the world.

Much of my energy goes into my creative and entreprenuerial projects in addition to health/self-improvement and spending time with my friends and family. I hope you enjoy what I have to share and are inspired by what I have to offer! ☆★★

Stuff I do

While I am traveling (and also back at home), I work with small business owners and managers to develop their brand identities. My roles vary based on the project, but key responsibilities usually include: logo design, overall identity development, website design and development, SEO and social media management. I am also the Co-Founder and CEO of NECESSIVEND based out of Charleston, SC and the Director of Brand Strategy and founding member of the start-up BREWPUBLIK based out of Charlotte, NC.
